Kanelboller

Description

This is a recipe for delicious Norwegian cinnamon rolls. Any cafe you walk into in Norway will serve some version of cinnamon rolls. They are delicious by themselves or with a cup of coffee

After moving from Norway to the US, my longing for traditional Norwegian recipes has grown. I want to learn how to make traditional Norwegian recipes so I can share them with my family in the US.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add all the dry ingredients together
  2. Add in milk carefully while stirring
  3. Knead dough
