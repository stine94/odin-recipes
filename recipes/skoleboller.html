Skoleboller

Description

Skoleboller, sometimes called "Skolebrod" is another traditoinal Norwegian baking recipe. Any cafe in Norway will sell Skoleboller - even most grocery stores will have them. The base recipe is the same as for 'boller' and then you create a delicious custard to go in the middle. Finish it by adding a powdered sugar glaze sprinkled with coconut on top. Kind of interesting how this is such a traditional Norwegian food when coconut is not at all native to Norway...

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Instant Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Cardamom
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Vanilly pastery cream
  • Powdered sugar
  • Shredded coconut

Steps

  1. Prepare pastry cream and place in the fridge to cool
  2. Heat the milk until it is slightly warm, add active dry yeast and sugar. Stir, and leae it for 5-10 minutes
  3. Add salt, remaining sugar, caradmom, and egg to a large bowl and mix well. Add to egg mixture.
  4. Slowly add the flour and softened butter, mixing with a wooden spoon until the dough starts to come together
  5. Knead until smooth and elastic
